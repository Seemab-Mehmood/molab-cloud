Deployment note

Membership accounts exist only from roster uploads here — there is no public self-registration endpoint. This panel is gated by (1) a private, unlisted URL path, (2) admin email/password auth, and optionally (3) a shared secret header. For real production use, also put this behind a VPN or IP allowlist and enable 2FA.

Upload membership roster

.xlsx, .xls, or .csv with columns: Name, MOLAB ID, Email, Hospital/Institution, Country, and optionally Tier. New rows are activated immediately — members log in with just their name and MOLAB ID, no password or email step. Each new membership starts a 12-month clock. Existing MOLAB IDs/emails are updated in place (their status and membership dates are untouched).
