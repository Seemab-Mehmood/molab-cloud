Membership is by invitation

MOLAB Cloud is a closed platform for healthcare professionals and researchers holding an active MOLAB membership. There is no public self-registration — accounts are created by the MOLAB Team from an institutional roster, and different membership tiers apply. Your MOLAB Membership ID is issued to you directly by the MOLAB Team; log in with that ID and your full name, no password required.

If your institution should be part of the network, or you believe you should already have access, contact the MOLAB Team:
